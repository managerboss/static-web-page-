About MUST

Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST) is a public university located in the Southern Highlands of Tanzania. It is dedicated to fostering scientific and technological innovation for socio-economic transformation.

MUST offers quality training, research, and consultancy services. The university is committed to producing highly competent professionals who can meet the demands of a fast-evolving global environment.

  • Master of Engineering Management
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Technical Education

Admissions

Admission to MUST is open to students who meet the minimum qualifications as outlined by TCU and NACTE regulations. Both Tanzanian and international students are welcome to apply.

  • Certificate and Diploma Admissions: CSEE with passes in key subjects including Mathematics and Science.
